Frozen Hamburgers

Feeling worn out? All the work's already done for you with frozen hamburgers. Pair with frozen french fries for a real crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • Frozen hamburger patties, one box
  • Hamburger buns
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat your barbeque on medium-high heat.
  2. Place the frozen patties on the barbeque. Turn after nine minutes so that they're cooked all the way through.
  3. Place the cooked patty on a bun. Add ketchup since it's the only condiment my kids will eat.
