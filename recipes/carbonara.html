Spaghetti Carbonara

A Creamy and Delicious Pasta

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic recipe. The sauce needs only two ingredients. Add pancetta or bacon to round out the dish. Garnish with pepper and some grated cheese. It's so simple!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • 2 Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese, 1 cup grated
  • Bacon, 1 rasher

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon and bring a pot of water to a boil for the pasta.
  2. Once the water is boiling, drop in the spaghetti. After the tips have softened, about 1 minute, stir the pasta to submerge all the spaghetti.
  3. Combine the eggs and grated cheese to form the sauce.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, about 9 minutes, transfer to a pan. You want to include a splash of the pasta water while transfering. I normally grab it with tongs and transfer it dripping wet rather than strain the pasta.
  5. Mix the pasta and sauce. Add the bacon.
