Simple Stirfry

A stirfry with chicken and broccoli

This stirfry comes together quickly and uses just a few ingredients. Perfect for feeding the family after a busy day!

Ingredients

  • Instant noodles
  • 2 Chicken breasts
  • Cashews, 100 grams
  • Olive oil, 1 tbsp
  • Black bean sauce, 2 tbsp
  • Red wine vinegar, 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Put the cashews in your frying pan over low heat.
  2. Start boiling a pot of water to cook the noodles.
  3. Take a knife and score the chicken breast lengthwise. Mix in a bowl with 1 tbsp each of the oil, vinegar, and black bean sauce.
  4. Remove the cashews from the frying pan. Put some oil in the pan and fry the chicken on high heat. Turn the chicken after a few minutes so it cooks all the way through.
  5. While the chicken is cooking, chop the broccoli into bite-size pieces.
  6. Reserve a few cashews for garnish. Crush the rest.
  7. Once the water comes to a boil, add the instant noodles. Wait about one minute and add the broccoli to the pot.
  8. Remove the cooked chicken from the frying pan and slice it.
  9. Mix the noodles, broccoli, and crushed cashews in the frying pan over low heat. Add another tablespoon of black bean sauce.
  10. Once fully mixed, serve the stirfry. Add the chicken and whole cashews on top.
